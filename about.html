Our Story

Established on Purpose

Adveris Advisors LLP was established in Bengaluru with a clear and singular purpose: to democratise access to high-quality legal and compliance services for businesses across India.

We saw a gap in the market quality advisory was often reserved for large corporations with deep pockets. We built Adveris to change that, bringing enterprise-grade legal expertise to startups, SMEs, and growing companies at every stage of their journey.

Today, we serve clients across sectors including technology, manufacturing, financial services, real estate, and e-commerce all under one roof, with a team that truly understands the Indian regulatory landscape.
