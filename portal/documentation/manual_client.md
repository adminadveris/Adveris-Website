# Adveris Advisors Portal: End-to-End Client Manual (v1.2)

## 1. Transparency & Privacy Governance
Your portal access is restricted to the "L3 - External Client" clearance. This ensures that your organization's data (PAN, GSTIN, Mandates, and Documents) is strictly isolated and invisible to any other client on the platform.

### 1.1 Secure Session
*   **30-Minute Timeout**: For your data's protection, the system will log you out after 30 minutes of inactivity.
*   **Warning**: A warning appears at 28 minutes. Click "Stay Logged In" to extend your access.

### 1.2 Real-Time Updates
*   **Notifications**: The bell icon in the header will pulse whenever:
    *   Adveris verifies and activates your request.
    *   A mandate is marked as "Completed."
    *   A professional is assigned to your project.

## 2. Tab-by-Tab Functional Breakdown

### 2.1 Tab: New Request (Engagement Initiation)
This is your primary tool for starting a new advisory project.

*   **Step 1: Identity Verification (PAN)**:
    *   **Process**: Enter your 10-digit Permanent Account Number.
    *   **Scenario A (Existing Account)**: If you have worked with us before, we will automatically load your details (Name, GSTIN, Address).
    *   **Scenario B (New Account)**: You will be prompted to fill in your full entity details.
*   **Step 2: Scoping (Requirements)**:
    *   **Field: Service Domain**: Select the high-level category (e.g., FDI, Tax).
    *   **Field: Technical Sub-Services**: Pick specific items from the dynamic checklist.
    *   **Field: Description**: Provide a narrative of your requirements.
*   **Step 3: Evidence Staging (Documents)**:
    *   **Validation**: Max 3 files.
    *   **Validation**: Max 5MB per file (PDF, PNG, JPG).
    *   **Validation**: This is the only time you can upload scoping documents for this specific request.

### 2.2 Tab: My Mandates (The Strategic Ledger)
Track all your past and current projects in one place.

*   **Search Engine**: Search by Request Reference Number or Service Type.
*   **Status Tracking**:
    *   **Pending**: Adveris is currently reviewing your documentation.
    *   **Active**: Your project is live. You can now see the name of the **Assigned Professional**.
    *   **Completed**: The final deliverables have been archived.
*   **History Audit**: Click the pulse icon on any row to see the exact timeline of when your mandate was reviewed, assigned, and processed.

### 2.3 Tab: Profile (Security & Identity)
*   **Update Validation**: You can update your contact phone number and gender. 
*   **Locking**: Your organizational role and account linkage are managed by Adveris Admins and cannot be changed by the client to maintain institutional integrity.
