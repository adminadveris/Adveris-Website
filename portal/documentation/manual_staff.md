# Adveris Advisors Portal: End-to-End Professional Staff Manual (v1.2)

## 1. Professional Identity & Security
Your role is defined as "Employee" within the "L2 - Operational Authority" clearance. You are the primary executor of firm mandates.

### 1.1 Access Governance
*   **Secure Session**: 30-minute auto-signout.
*   **Extension**: A glass-UI warning appears at 28 minutes. You **must** click "Stay Logged In" to avoid losing progress on large timesheets or expense forms.
*   **Profile**: Your **Designation** and **Department** are visible to the Admin team. Ensure these are correct to receive appropriate mandate assignments.

### 1.2 Communication (Notifications)
*   **Real-Time Alerts**: You will receive a notification whenever:
    *   An Admin assigns you to a new mandate.
    *   One of your expenses is approved or rejected.
    *   A client submits documents for your active mandate.

## 2. Tab-by-Tab Functional Breakdown

### 2.1 Tab: Overview (Your Daily Pulse)
*   **Operational Snapshot**: View your logged hours for the current week vs. previous weeks.
*   **KPIs**: See how many active mandates you are currently managing.

### 2.2 Tab: My Mandates (Operational Queue)
*   **What you see**: A filtered list of requests where you are the "Assigned Professional."
*   **Search/Filter**: Search by Client Name or Mandate ID.
*   **Functional Detail**:
    *   **Internal Notes (Update)**: You are required to update "Internal Progress Notes" regularly.
    *   **Client Communication**: View notes provided by the client during the intake phase.
    *   **Audit**: View the history of status changes on your projects.

### 2.3 Tab: Timesheets (The Weekly Ledger)
*   **Process**: Use the 7-day grid to log your billable hours.
*   **Functional Steps**:
    1.  Select Account -> Select Mandate ID.
    2.  Enter Hours (e.g., `4.5`).
    3.  Enter mandatory "Activity Notes" for each row.
*   **Strict Validations**:
    *   **Future Locking**: You cannot log time for future dates.
    *   **Required Fields**: A row cannot be saved without an Account, Mandate, and at least one hour entry.
    *   **Persistence**: Click **"Save Weekly Ledger"** to sync with the database.

### 2.4 Tab: Expenses (Reimbursement Management)
*   **Submission Process**:
    1.  Enter Amount, Date, and Category (Travel, Filing, etc.).
    2.  **Evidence Staging**: You are required to upload supporting receipts.
        *   **Validation**: Max 3 files per expense.
        *   **Validation**: Max 5MB per file.
*   **Lifecycle Scenarios**:
    *   **Submitted**: Editable. You can fix mistakes here.
    *   **Approved**: **LOCKED**. You can no longer edit this record. It is now part of the firm's financial audit trail.
    *   **Rejected**: View the "Admin Feedback" in the record notes and re-submit if necessary.

### 2.5 Tab: Accounts & Clients (CRM View)
*   **Function**: View-only access to the institutional registry.
*   **Search**: Locate a client's address, GSTIN, or contact person.
*   **Update**: You can update client contact details but cannot change the master Account PAN (this is an Admin-only privilege).

### 2.6 Tab: New Request (Internal Enrollment)
*   **Function**: Register a mandate for a client you are onboarding.
*   **Process**: Identical to the Client workflow, but you have the privilege to skip the PAN check if the account is already known.
